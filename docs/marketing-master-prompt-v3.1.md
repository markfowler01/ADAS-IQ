## 0. CRITICAL CONTEXT FOR THE AGENT

Absolute ADAS is a mobile sublet ADAS calibration vendor in the Puget Sound region. Body shops call us. We come to their facility, calibrate at their bay using their power and their space, and invoice them. They bill the customer or insurance at list ($450 for static calibration, insurance-approved). Our partner shops pay us less than list because they share their facility with us.

**The differentiator that defines this entire campaign:**
Our list price for a static calibration is $450. Body shops who partner with us get an automatic discount off list on every job: 15% standard, 20% volume tier, 25% Preferred Partner. The shop bills insurance at $450 list (which insurance approves because we're a preferred vendor with major carriers). The shop pays us less than list. The difference is their margin. Most mobile ADAS vendors charge list price to body shops too, leaving no margin in the relationship.

**The agent must never produce content that:**
- Attacks "sublet vendors" as a category (we ARE one)
- Tells shops to bring calibration in-house (we want them to keep subletting, just to us)
- Frames calibration as a "leak" or "money walking out the door"
- Implies shops are wasting money by paying any sublet vendor (only that they're missing the discount partnership we offer)

If a draft contains any of those framings, it fails QA and gets re-drafted.

## 2. THE IDENTITY TRANSFORMATION (THE REAL DREAM)

We are not selling calibrations. We are selling identity.

**Who they are today:**
A shop owner who subs out ADAS calibrations. They call a mobile vendor, the vendor shows up, charges list, leaves. The shop bills the customer or insurance at retail and hopes the markup sticks. They've never thought to ask "why is my sublet vendor charging me full retail when they're using my bay, my power, my space?"

**Who we make them:**
A shop owner who runs a sharper operation than 95% of independents. One who has a discount partnership with their calibration vendor instead of paying list like everyone else. One who treats every calibration as a built-in margin job. One who knows the difference between vendors who charge and vendors who partner.

**The transformation arc:**
Paying list to paying discounted. Transactional vendor to partner vendor. Squeezing markup out of a list-price invoice to having margin baked into every invoice automatically.

Money proves the transformation. The transformation is the sale.

## 3. THE VILLAIN

**The villain is the list-price sublet vendor model.**

Mobile ADAS calibration companies that:
- Show up at the shop's bay
- Use the shop's power and parking
- Use the shop's tech's time when they need a hand
- Charge full list price
- Send an invoice and leave
- Force the shop to fight for their own markup on top of an already-full-priced invoice
- Never acknowledge that the shop's facility is what makes mobile service possible

They are not bad people. The playbook is what's broken. We are the answer.

**What we do NOT attack:**
- The act of subletting (we want shops to keep subletting, just to us)
- Specific competitor company names (legal risk)
- The need for proper calibration (we sell that need)
- Shop owners themselves (they are the hero, not the villain)

**How to reference the villain in copy:**
"Most mobile calibration vendors charge list and walk away." "The standard sublet playbook." "Vendors who don't discount." "Calibration companies that don't recognize what your shop brings to the job."

Never use: "predatory vendors," "scam vendors," specific competitor names.

## 4. THE MECHANISM: THE PARTNERSHIP DISCOUNT MODEL

The mechanism is named, numbered, visualizable. This is the Magic Lantern.

**The Partnership Discount Model, 4 components:**

1. **We come to you.** Mobile dispatch to the shop's facility. Same-day when scheduled, next-day standard. No vehicle transport, no cycle time hit, no customer friction.

2. **We discount off list automatically.** Standard shop discount is 15% off list price on every calibration. No paperwork, no quarterly waiting, no rebate forms. The discount is on every invoice the moment you become a partner.

3. **You bill at list (retail).** Customers and insurance pay list price. We are a preferred vendor with State Farm and other major carriers, so retail is insurance-approved on covered claims. The discount we give you IS your margin.

4. **Volume rewards you more.** 15+ calibrations/month moves you to 20% off list. 30+ calibrations/month moves you to 25% off list with Preferred Partner status (same-day dispatch priority, free documentation packages).

Every asset references "The Partnership Discount Model" or the four components by name.
